## A practice, not a finish line / 一种实践，而不是终点

Constant learning is the habit of returning to unfinished questions with a little more patience and a little more evidence.

持续学习，是带着更多耐心和证据，重新回到那些尚未完成的问题。

This blog is where I keep notes from that process: technical experiments, books that change how I see a problem, and small observations that become useful only after they meet practice.

这里记录这个过程中的笔记：技术实验、改变问题视角的书，以及只有进入实践后才真正有用的小观察。

## What I will keep / 我会持续记录什么

- **Build / 构建**: make small tools, test assumptions, and explain the decisions.
- **Learn / 学习**: turn new concepts into working examples instead of collecting definitions.
- **Think / 思考**: revisit ideas over time and let experience revise them.

The notes will be bilingual whenever that helps the idea travel further. English gives me a wider technical vocabulary; Chinese gives me a closer and more precise way to reflect.

当双语能让想法走得更远时，我会同时使用中文和英文。英文提供更广的技术语汇，中文让我更贴近、更准确地回望经验。

There is no final version of this notebook. Each post is a checkpoint: a clear enough record of what I understand today, and an invitation to learn what I do not understand yet.

这本笔记没有最终版本。每篇文章都是一个检查点：记录今天已经理解的内容，也提醒自己继续学习尚未理解的部分。
